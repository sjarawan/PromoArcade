About Us

The Story Behind PromoArcade

We believe the best marketing doesn't feel like marketing — it feels like play.

What We Do

PromoArcade builds lightweight, mobile-first web games designed specifically for business promotion. We help brands turn their campaigns, events, and promotions into shareable, interactive experiences that customers genuinely enjoy.

Every game we create is hosted, branded, and ready to share — no apps to download, no complicated tech for you to manage. Just a clean link your audience can tap, play, and share.

Whether it's a seasonal campaign, a grand opening, a loyalty reward, or a social media push, we help businesses stand out with something memorable — a game their audience actually wants to play.

What Drives Us

We focus on four things: creativity, speed, engagement, and simplicity. We keep things lean, move fast, and build experiences that are fun first — because fun is what gets shared, and sharing is what drives results.
